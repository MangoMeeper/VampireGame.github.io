Strength

Strength represents muscle power, durability, certain forms of athletic ability, and overall fitness. You make a Strength roll to resist an effect that forces you to move or that restrains you, as well as when you climb, swim, attack with most hand-to-hand weapons, kick down a door, or overpower another creature. You also use Strength to withstand or fight off the effects of pain, injury, poison, or infection.

Agility

Agility encompasses poise, dexterity, balance, and reflexes. You make an Agility roll when you want to keep your balance, perhaps because someone has just shoved you or you are moving across challenging terrain. Agility also comes into play when you would try to accomplish a task that requires manual dexterity, such as picking a lock, disarming a trap, or performing an act of legerdemain.
